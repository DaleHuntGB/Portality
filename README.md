# Portalist

**Portalist** is a lightweight travel menu for **World of Warcraft Retail** that puts your most useful teleport options behind one quick dropdown.

Open the menu with a keybind and Portalist will show the travel tools you have chosen and can currently use, including:

- Challenge Mode dungeon teleports
- Hearthstones and hearthstone-style toys
- Engineering wormholes
- Teleport and portal spells
- Mailbox toys

The dropdown opens at your cursor, hides after use, and shows cooldown progress directly on each entry.

## Features

- One compact dropdown for your travel options
- Configurable list of enabled destinations and toys
- Support for spells and toys in the same menu
- Cooldown timer and status bar on each button
- Challenge Mode portal configuration grouped by expansion
- Keybind support with two binding slots
- Slash commands for opening the configuration window
- Only shows entries that are both enabled and currently usable

## Categories

Portalist lets you enable entries from these groups:

- **Challenge Mode Portals**
- **Hearthstones**
- **Wormholes**
- **Portals**
- **Mailboxes**

Unavailable entries are still visible in the config window, but they are disabled until your character learns the spell or owns the toy.

## How To Use

1. Install the addon and reload your UI.
2. Type `/portalist` or `/port` to open the configuration window.
3. Enable the portals, hearthstones, wormholes, and mailbox toys you want in the dropdown.
4. Assign the `PORTALIST_OPEN` keybind either in WoW's Key Bindings menu or from the Portalist config window.
5. Press your keybind to open the dropdown at your cursor, then click the destination you want to use.

## Slash Commands

- `/portalist`
- `/port`

Both commands open the configuration window.

## Compatibility

- **Game**: World of Warcraft Retail
- **Interface**: `120000`

## Notes

- The dropdown only includes entries you have manually enabled in Portalist.
- An entry must also be usable before it appears in the menu.
- Spell availability depends on your class, spec, faction, and unlocks.
- Toy availability depends on owning the toy and being able to use it on the current character.
- Challenge Mode portals are organized by expansion in the config window to make seasonal teleports easier to manage.

## Why Portalist?

Portalist is built for fast travel without a bulky UI:

- one keybind
- one dropdown
- the travel options you actually care about
